What the ROSE Network Is

The ROSE Network connects Intelligence Corps personnel and veterans with a trusted community of professionals across industry, government, and the private sector.

Through mentoring, professional introductions, and industry insight, the network helps members prepare for civilian careers and remain connected throughout their professional lives.
