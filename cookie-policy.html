Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and why.

Last updated: 20 March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to site owners. Some cookies are essential for the website to function; others help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

We also use localStorage, a browser-based storage mechanism, to remember your cookie consent preference.

2. How We Use Cookies

This website uses a consent-first approach. No analytics or marketing cookies are set until you actively click "Accept" on our cookie consent banner. If you reject cookies or take no action, only essential functionality operates.

3. Cookie Categories

3.1 Essential (Always Active)

These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.

Storage Purpose Duration
cookie_consent (localStorage) Remembers your cookie consent choice so the banner is not shown repeatedly. Until you clear browser data

3.2 Analytics (Requires Consent)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users by assigning a randomly generated number. 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Maintains session state for Google Analytics 4. 2 years
_gid Google Analytics Distinguishes users for analytics reporting. 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics Throttles request rate to limit data collection on high-traffic sites. 1 minute

3.3 Marketing / Tracking (Requires Consent)

These cookies are set by third-party services we use and may track your browsing activity to help us understand engagement.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
__hssc HubSpot Tracks sessions. Used to determine if HubSpot should increment the session number and timestamps in the __hstc cookie. 30 minutes
__hssrc HubSpot Set to determine if the user has restarted their browser. If this cookie does not exist when HubSpot manages cookies, it is considered a new session. Session
__hstc HubSpot Tracks visitors. Contains the domain, initial timestamp, last timestamp, current timestamp, and session number. 6 months
hubspotutk HubSpot Keeps track of a visitor's identity. Passed to HubSpot on form submission and used to de-duplicate contacts. 6 months

4. Google Consent Mode v2

Our website implements Google Consent Mode v2, which ensures that Google services (Analytics, Ads) respect your consent choices. When you reject cookies:

  • No analytics data is collected by Google.
  • No advertising cookies are set.
  • No user data is shared with Google advertising services.
  • URL passthrough is enabled for basic attribution without cookies.
  • Ads data redaction is enabled for additional privacy protection.

5. Google Tag Manager

We use Google Tag Manager (GTM) to manage the analytics scripts on our site. GTM itself does not set cookies or collect personal data. It is a container that fires other tags (such as Google Analytics) based on your consent preferences.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Using our cookie settings: Click the "Cookie Settings" link in the website footer to re-open the consent banner and change your choice.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites.
  • Clearing localStorage: You can clear your browser's local storage to reset your consent preference. In most browsers, this is found under Settings > Privacy > Clear Browsing Data.

Browser-specific guidance

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies

7. Third-Party Cookies

Our website links to external sites (ICA, Veterans Gateway, SSAFA, Royal British Legion, COBSEO) which may set their own cookies. We have no control over these cookies and recommend you review the cookie policies of these sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for regulatory reasons. Changes will be published on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at ica_hq@roseandlaurel.uk.

For more information about how we handle personal data, see our Privacy Policy.
